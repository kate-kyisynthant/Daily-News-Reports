Who are the Kardashians-Jenners family?

The Kardashian family, also referred to as the Kardashian-Jenner family,is an American family prominent in the fields of entertainment, reality television, fashion design, and business. It consists of their children Kourtney, Kim, Khloé, and Rob Kardashian, as well as their grandchildren. After Robert and Kris' divorce in 1991, Kris married Olympic decathlon champion Bruce Jenner (now Caitlyn Jenner) with whom she had two daughters: Kendall and Kylie Jenner. Notable extended relatives include Kendall and Kylie's half-siblings, Brandon and Brody Jenner.
