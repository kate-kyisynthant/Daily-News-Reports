'Enola Holmes 2' cleverly brings
the public attention to
Millie Bobby Brown.

Millie Bobby Brown is widely known for her wonderful capacity of performing remarkable roles especially at a young age. Despite that, the actress positively surprised the public in the first Netflix “Enola Holmes” movie, in 2020, when she showed her talents.

As the positive feedbacks from the first movie exceeded the expectations, it was logical to consider a continuation for an immediate success. In Enola Holmes 2, not only the actress played her role well, but also gained the public again with her amazing charisma and talent. With that, we can say Millie is fated and perfect to act in this movie.
